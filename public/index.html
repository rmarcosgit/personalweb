Biography

I am a Geographer, interested in forest fire modeling and multiscale risk assessment.

I completed my PhD in Environmental and Land management at the University of Zaragoza in 2015. I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow (JdlC - Formación) at the Department of Agricultural and Forest Engineering, Universityof LLeida. I am also funding member of RemOT-Technologies S.L.
